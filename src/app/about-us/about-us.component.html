About Pet Shree

There’s nothing as exciting as getting a wholesome package of pet services with just a click. Pet Shree is a web app that provides the easiest way for you and your pets to access high-quality animal care and other pet-related services.

So if you’re looking for a means of pampering your dogs and cats or you just want to share some of your special pet moments with other pet lovers and pet care providers, Pet Shree is the go-to app that provides the desired outcomes.

How Pet Shree Help

Pet Shree brings positive energy for pet owners to revamp the health goals for their dogs and cats using technology as leverage. The app supports with the following;

  • Health management: Who says those lovely four-legged creatures don’t need medical attention? To keep them vibrant, they need regular health checkups and vaccinations just like humans. This is where Pet Shree comes in to help you manage your pet care by keeping track of records like vaccination, deworming, vet appointments, grooming, and other pet-care services.

  • Listings: The app helps you to find and book pet-related services (alongside their requirements) within the nearby region. It provides complete and verified profiles of Veterinary doctors and other service providers like groomers, trainers, and boarders. The bookings come with cost-effective packages, and the rating of our enlisted service providers offer reliable feedback for prospective clients.

  • Other Consultations: With the Pet Shree, pet owners can consult our pet professionals online even if they need additional consultancy services like inquiries on food and nutrition, behavior and training, pet travel or pet parenting.
